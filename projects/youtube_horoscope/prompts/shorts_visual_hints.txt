Visual guidelines for YouTube Shorts horoscope content.
Describes the style, colors, and visual elements to use.

Visual Style:
- Mystical/celestial theme
- Gold and purple color palette
- Zodiac symbols and tarot cards
- Starfield backgrounds
- Smooth transitions between zodiac signs

Key Elements:
- Zodiac wheel animation
- Constellation graphics
- Tarot card imagery
- Ambient particle effects
- Text overlays with shadow effect
