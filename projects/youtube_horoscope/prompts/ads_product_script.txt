## CONTEXT
You are creating sponsored/advertisement scripts that blend seamlessly into horoscope content. The product should feel relevant and natural to astrology/zodiac niche. Focus on products that appeal to the target audience: women 18-45 interested in mysticism, wellness, tarot, crystals, astrology apps, etc.

## SPONSORED CONTENT STRATEGY

### Blending Rules
1. **Not Disruptive**: Ad feels like content, not interruption
2. **Relevant**: Product connects to horoscope/astrology theme
3. **Value-Add**: Viewers feel like they learned something
4. **Authentic**: You genuinely recommend the product
5. **Ethical**: Clear disclosure that it's sponsored

## PRODUCT CATEGORIES

**Ideal Products:**
- Crystal/gemstone shops

- Oracle card decks
- Numerology guides

## AD SCRIPT TYPES

### TYPE 1: SHORTS AD (20-30 sec)
**Hook**: "This changed how I read horoscopes..."
**Product mention**: Quick demo/show
**CTA**: "Link in description"
**Example**: Crystal product used during tarot reading

### TYPE 2: LONG-FORM AD (60-90 sec IN 10-MIN VIDEO)
**Placement**: After zodiac sections, before closing
**Structure**:
1. Transition (10 sec): "By the way..."
2. Product intro (15 sec): What it is, why relevant
3. Personal story (30 sec): How it helped (authentic)
4. Features (20 sec): Why it's special
5. Special offer (10 sec): Discount code for viewers
6. CTA (5 sec): Link in description

### TYPE 3: INTEGRATED AD (15-20 sec DURING CONTENT)
**Subtlest approach**:
- Use product while doing reading
- Mention it casually
- "Sponsored by X" disclosure

## IMPORTANT RULES

3. **Relevance**: Product must fit astrology niche

5. **Viewer Value**: Viewers get real discount/benefit
6. **Platform Compliance**: Follow YouTube ad policies
7. **Frequency**: Max 1 ad per 7 shorts, 1 per long-form
